---
title: What this website should hold
description: A working note on the kinds of things worth keeping on a personal site.
date: 2026-03-30
---

The goal is not to make a perfect personal brand page.

The goal is to make a useful place on the internet that answers a few honest questions:

- what I am building
- what I am learning
- what I have written down so far
- how someone can reach me

That means the website should stay light.

A short introduction, a few current signals, some writing, and clear contact links are enough for version one. The rest can arrive later if it earns its place.
