This website is starting over, but the story behind it is not.

I wanted the repo history to stay real even while the old filler site disappeared. The date trail matters to me because it shows when the work actually began, not just when the polished version finally showed up.

So this rebuild is intentionally simple: Astro, GitHub Pages, a writing-first layout, and space for ideas that are still moving.

I do not want this site to feel frozen. If a section becomes unhelpful, I would rather change it than pretend it still belongs here.
